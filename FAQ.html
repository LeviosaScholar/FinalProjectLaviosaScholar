Frequently Asked Question

Who is elligible to apply for scholarship?

All undergraduate or postgraduate IIUM students are encouraged to apply if they fulfill the elligibility requirements. The students must maintain CGPA 3.50 and above in every semester in their studies and being active in co-curricular activities.

How do I apply for scholarship?

You can apply online scholarship by fill in the form in registration and submission pages. You need to submit your identification, profile picture, offer letter, certificate photos and parents annual salary slip.

What fields of programme does this scholarship website offers?

This website offers different types of scholarship for various of field programmes for every Kulliyyah that exist in IIUM. We offer local scholarship for Malaysian students and international scholarship for our international students. We believe in preserving the rights to get excellent quality of education.

When can I know the result of application status?

You can know the result of aplication status 1 month after the application date closed. You will receive an email from your respective Kulliyyah of your application status.

Can I resubmit my application again if previous application unsuccessful?

Unfortunately IIUM Scholarship does not accept any applications resubmitted within the current semester. However, you may apply scholarship application again for next semester.
